Alternative OUYA Launcher by FrankkieNL

Download APK: https://raw.github.com/frankkienl/OuyaLauncherFrankkieNL/master/FrankkieOuyaLauncher/FrankkieOuyaLauncher.apk

Want your normal OUYA Launcher back? Just uninstall this app. Go to Advanced Settings, Apps, FrankkieOuyaLauncher, click Uninstall

Note: you must install SuperUser or SuperSu to turn off the console via this Launcher. (Or just press the power button, or open the stock launcher)
